ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Vie, a web designer who thrives at the crossroads of logic and creativity. My work reflects my passion for both the technical precision of code and the artistry of design. As a lifelong pixel game enthusiast and artist, I bring the same attention to detail and playfulness from gaming into creating intuitive, visually striking websites. I love crafting digital experiences where technology meets imagination, bridging function and form. When I’m not coding or designing, you’ll probably find me exploring how to turn ideas into reality—whether through art or tech experiments.
